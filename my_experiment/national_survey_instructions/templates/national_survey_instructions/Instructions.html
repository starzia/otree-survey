Next we will present you questions asked in a series of national American surveys in recent years. Some are fact-based questions. Some are opinion-based questions. For each question, you will be asked to respond to a question asking you to pick between two options, A & B. You will make your selection on a 7-point scale to indicate your preference for option A or B. The responses of previous participants in a previous survey will be visible to you next to each option, but you can feel free to ignore this information. Specifically, the percentage of previous participants that prefer a particular option will be listed in parentheses next to each one (if percentages don't add up to 100 it is because there was a no-response or another option available).
